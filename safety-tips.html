Traffic Safety Tips

Here are some important traffic safety tips to keep in mind:

  • Always wear your seatbelt, no matter how short the journey.
  • Follow speed limits and avoid aggressive driving.
  • Do not use your phone while driving. Use hands-free if necessary.
  • Keep a safe distance from the vehicle in front of you.
  • Be extra cautious in bad weather or poor visibility conditions.
