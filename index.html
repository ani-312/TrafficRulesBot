Traffic Rules

Welcome to the traffic rules website! Here are some important rules you must follow while driving:

  • Speed Limits: Follow the speed limits as per local regulations.
  • Traffic Signals: Red means stop, yellow means prepare to stop, and green means go.
  • Pedestrian Crossings: Stop for pedestrians at zebra crossings.
  • Seatbelts: Always wear your seatbelt while driving.
  • No Drunk Driving: Do not drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
  • Use of Indicators: Signal when turning or changing lanes.
  • Lane Discipline: Stick to your lane and overtake properly.
